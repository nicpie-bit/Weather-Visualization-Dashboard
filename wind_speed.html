Wind Speed

Looking at the graph for Wind Speeds, there seems to be a trend that the wind speeds are mainly bewteen 0-10mph no matter if you are close to the equator or not. The northern hemisphere more than the south has a consistently low wind speed across all latitudes. The southern hemisphere's wind speeds are more scattered.
